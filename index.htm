MighTea Boba Complete Beverage Guide _________________ Iced Bubble Tea - Milk Tea MighTea Size (12oz) 1 Measure 12oz of tea base. 2 Add 1 scoop of creamer. 3 Add 1.5 scoops of flavor powder. 4 Add 2 scoops of bobas. 5 Add 1 full pump of sugar syrup. 6 Shake well and serve over ice. Teany Size (8oz) 1 Measure 8oz of tea base. 2 Add 1 scoop of creamer. 3 Add 1 scoop of flavor powder. 4 Add 2 scoops of bobas. 5 Add 1/2 pump of sugar syrup. 6 Shake well and serve over ice. Iced Bubble Tea - Fruit Tea MighTea Size (12oz) 1 Measure 12oz of tea base. 2 Do not add creamer. 3 Add 1.5 pumps of flavor syrup. 4 Add 2 scoops of bobas. 5 Add 1 full pump of sugar syrup. 6 Shake well and serve over ice. Teany Size (8oz) 1 Measure 8oz of tea base. 2 Do not add creamer. 3 Add 1 pump of flavor syrup. 4 Add 2 scoops of bobas. 5 Add 1/2 pump of sugar syrup. 6 Shake well and serve over ice. Bubble Tea Smoothie (MighTea Only) Milk Tea Smoothie Type 1 Measure 10oz of tea. 2 Add 1 scoop of creamer. 3 Add 2 scoops of flavor powder. 4 Add 1 full pump of sugar syrup. 5 Add 2 cups of ice to the blender. 6 Blend until smooth. 7 Add 2 scoops of bobas to the cup and pour in the smoothie. Fruit Tea Smoothie Type 1 Measure 8oz of tea. 2 Do not add creamer. 3 Add 2 pumps of flavor syrup. 4 Add 1 full pump of sugar syrup. 5 Add 2 cups of ice to the blender. 6 Blend until smooth. 7 Add 2 scoops of bobas to the cup and pour in the smoothie. Honeycomb Bubble Tea (MighTea Only) Preparation Steps 1 Measure 12oz of milk. 2 Add 1.5 pumps of Wintermelon fruit syrup. 3 Add 2 scoops of Tapioca bobas. 4 Add 1 full pump of sugar syrup. 5 Stir well with a spoon. 6 Note: DO NOT SHAKE (Milk will foam too much). Specialty Sodas & Spritzers (MighTea Only) Standard Base Instructions 1 Fill MighTea cup almost all the way with ice. 2 Add flavor syrup (see specific recipe below). 3 Pour the full 12oz can of soda on top. 4 Quick stir with an espresso spoon. 5 Top with a splash of half & half. 6 Cap the cup and swirl. Signature Lattes MighTea Size (Hot or Iced) 1 Brew a triple shot of espresso. 2 Add sauces: • S'mores: 1 big scoop chocolate sauce. • Mounds: 1 big scoop chocolate sauce. • Jolly Cranberry: 1 big scoop white chocolate. 3 Add syrups: • S'mores: 6 pumps Toasted Marshmallow. • Mounds: 6 pumps Coconut. • Jolly Cranberry: 4 pumps Cranberry. 4 For Iced: Mix flavors into espresso until melted, add cold milk and ice. 5 For Hot: Steam milk with the syrup, pour over espresso and sauces. 6 Final Touches: • S'mores: Whip cream, chocolate drizzle, graham cracker sprinkles. • Mounds: Whip cream, chocolate drizzle, coconut sprinkles. • Jolly Cranberry: Whip cream. Specialty Milkshakes MighTea Size 1 Measure 6oz of milk. 2 Add 1.5 scoops white frappe powder. 3 Add specific flavors: • Strawberry: 2 scoops Strawberry powder. • Pomegranate: 2 pumps Pomegranate syrup. • Green Apple: 2 pumps Green Apple + 1 pump Lemon. • Peach Mango: 1 pump Peach + 1 pump Mango. • Blueberry: 2 pumps Blueberry syrup. • Blue Hawaiian: 2 scoops Blue Hawaiian powder. • Peppermint Mocha: 2 scoops Choc Mocha powder + 7 pumps Peppermint syrup. 4 Add 2.5 cups of ice. 5 Blend until smooth. 6 Add 1.5 to 2 scoops of boba/pearls to the cup. 7 Pour milkshake into cup. 8 Add toppings: Whip cream + specific garnishes (Gummy tree for peppermint, Gummy shark for Hawaiian, etc.). Teany Size 1 Measure 4oz of milk. 2 Add 1 scoop white frappe powder. 3 Add specific flavors: • Strawberry: 1.5 scoops Strawberry powder. • Pomegranate: 1.5 pumps Pomegranate syrup. • Green Apple: 1 pump Green Apple + 0.5 pump Lemon. • Peach Mango: 1 pump Peach + 0.5 pump Mango. • Blueberry: 1.5 pumps Blueberry syrup. • Blue Hawaiian: 1.5 scoops Blue Hawaiian powder. • Peppermint Mocha: 1.5 scoops Choc Mocha powder + 5 pumps Peppermint syrup. 4 Add 1.5 cups of ice. 5 Blend until smooth. 6 Add 1 scoop of boba/pearls to the cup. 7 Pour milkshake into cup. 8 Add toppings: Whip cream + specific garnishes. Real Fruit Smoothie Standard Instructions (Teany or MighTea) 1 Fill the cup (Teany or MighTea) completely with ice. 2 Fill the cup to the top with real fruit smoothie puree (Flavor: Strawberry, Mango, or Strawberry Banana). 3 Dump the contents into the blender. 4 Blend on setting 4 until smooth. 5 Pour back into the same cup. 6 Top with whipped cream if requested. Espresso Machine Operations Brewing Process 1 Select the correct scoop: Single for single shot, Double for double shot. 2 Use the grinder to fill the scoop to the line. 3 Use the Espresso Press to compact the grounds and level them off. 4 Insert the scoop into the machine and rotate clockwise to lock. 5 Place the espresso cup under the spout. 6 Push the Start button: 2nd from left for DOUBLE, far left for SINGLE. Steaming Process 1 Fill the steaming cup no more than half full with milk. 2 Insert the steamer wand and lift the lever. 3 Steam until the thermometer reads 140-160 degrees (110 for kids). 4 Clean the steamer wand immediately after use. 5 Discard used grounds and clean the machine.
